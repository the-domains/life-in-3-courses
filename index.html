A Poteger Garden

Potagers are French styled kitchen gardens that combine flowering ornamental plants with those grown for vegetables, fruits and herbs. A potager should be designed to please all the senses so it should be beautiful as well as functional. Most every French household, regardless of space constraints has a little potager….GL and I have had a potager for most of our married life. They have ranged in size from a few small pots on a ledge to full gardens with walking paths. We live in a condo in San Diego now and have placed our garden in pots around the outside of our home. Plenty of space to grow lavender,tomatoes, basil, peppers, geraniums, lettuce, cucumbers and our small orange and lime trees. How does your garden grow? We would love to see pictures and hear stories of your potager.

Health and Wellness

There comes a time in your life when your body begins to rebel against excess and neglect. Over the last few years my health declined to such a degree that when I wasn't working I was bedridden. Fibromyalgia, auto-immune disorder, and the inevitable depression that comes with these types of illnesses had made me a prisoner in my own body. I gained over 50 pounds and continued to decline. I finally came to the end of my rope. I could either quite my job and be fully disabled or I could take charge of my life. I decided I wasn't quite ready to give up on life.

Life in 3 Courses

Welcome to Life in 3 Courses.

We believe that life is a journey and each stage is unique and beautiful. After 50 you have an opportunity to approach life in a different way. You can dare to dream of new ways of being, of doing. Expectations of raising a family, societies pressures of doing the “right” thing whatever that is doesn’t seem quite so important. You have lived and learned and now can create your world and define your time on your terms. It doesn’t mean that we shirk our responsibilities, in fact for many of us the responsibilities increase as we face health issues, aging, and being squeezed between our obligations for parents and grandchildren. We hope that you will join us as we explore all of this and more.
